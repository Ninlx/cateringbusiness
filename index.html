PORK CALDERETA

The dish is similar to meat stews from the Iberian Peninsula and was brought to the Philippines by the Spaniards during their 300-year occupation of the Philippines.

LECHON

Lechon is derived from the Spanish word leche, with translates to milk. The origin of the dish used small young suckling pigs that were still nursing milk from their mothers.

PORK DINUGUAN

Dinuguan or also known as chocolate meat is a savory dish made with diced pork, blood, and spices.

PORK HUMBA

Humbà, is a Filipino braised pork dish originating from the Visayas Islands of the Philippines.

ABOUT US

All About iCREATE Catering

iCREATE Catering are committed to delivering quality service, building long term relationships with our clients, and exceeding their expectations.

iCREATE Catering understands that there are a wide variety of dietary needs in today’s society as well as varying budgets. We thrive off of providing menu options to meet any nutritional restrictions. Our variety in restaurant locations and cooking experience gives us the ability to supply our clients with phenomenal cuisine options. Whether it’s a light meal consisting of appetizers and desserts, a full-on banquet style buffet, or a multiple course meal, we can work within your financial limit to customize a menu for your corporate event. iCREATE Catering is here to surpass your expectations without extending your pocketbook.

As one of most established caterers, we are passionate about the delivery of high quality food and service. We are able to provide advice and assistance on all aspects of event planning and management, through our network of partners and preferred suppliers.

We are Wedding Catering Specialists, not just in Perth but throughout Country Western Australia with over 73 wedding parties to our name, some brides even choosing us more than once!

We offer our continued support for Breast Cancer and Prostate Cancer Research and associated charities, to assist in the fight against these terrible diseases.

We are also one of foremost, specialist corporate catering companies, providing all forms of catering service to the local corporate community, including breakfast meetings, working boardroom lunches, general meetings and retirement and Christmas parties.

For 2019-2020 we have redesigned our menus, to give a quality,contemporary dining experience. As part of our personal service, we can provide individually designed menus and are happy to meet clients to discuss their specific requirements. With iCREATE Catering, you can be assured of quality professional service and complete attention to detail.
